description:
  - Developed the creative concept and visual identity for a new restaurant, built
    around the idea of a contemporary post office. Working as part of a multidisciplinary
    team, we created a cohesive brand strategy that extended across every customer
    touchpoint, engaging all senses through visual design, scent, sound, and tactile
    experiences.
  - The project resulted in a comprehensive brand system, including the logo, menu,
    posters, postcards, and a concept book documenting the creative vision and development
    process. Every element was designed to create a memorable and immersive brand
    experience, ensuring consistency across both physical and printed materials.
