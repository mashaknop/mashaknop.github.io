description:
- SJI is a personalized gift box startup that needed an identity flexible enough to
  serve a wide range of customers, occasions, and price points — without driving up
  production costs.
- 'The challenge was to design a system, not just a logo: one capable of expressing
  near- infinite variation while remaining visually cohesive and easy to produce at
  scale. The solution centers on a geometric mark — an abstracted box — that acts
  as a structural canvas. Paired with a library of interchangeable, message-driven
  stickers, each package can feel tailored to the moment while the production process
  stays efficient and repeatable.'
- 'The result is a brand architecture built to grow with the business: new occasions,
  new audiences, new messages — all addressed by swapping a single modular element,
  rather than starting from scratch.'
