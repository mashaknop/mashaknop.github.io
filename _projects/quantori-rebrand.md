description:
  - A strong brand is built on a clear and consistent visual identity. The right visual
    language not only supports the story being told, but strengthens how it's understood
    and remembered. For this rebrand, the goal was to evolve the company's identity
    while preserving a sense of familiarity. Maintaining continuity with the existing
    brand ensured a smoother transition for both the company and its audience.
  - For Quantori, the rebrand centered around the existing logo, which became the
    foundation for the broader visual system. The mark was refined and simplified,
    establishing a cleaner, more modern direction while retaining its recognizable
    character.
  - A new color palette was developed through research into visual trends and conventions
    within the life sciences industry, helping position the brand within its space
    while creating a more distinctive identity. The circle element derived from the
    logo became a recurring motif across the system, influencing image treatments,
    graphic components, and the website experience to create a cohesive and recognizable
    brand language.
