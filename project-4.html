EDDesign Award

Deliverables:
Logo, Icons, Postcards, Packaging

Designing a visual identity for an architectural conference demanded a concept that could stand on its own logic. The system is grounded in a strict grid, with simple geometric forms acting as modular building blocks — echoing the underlying principles of architectural construction. Form follows concept: the same rules that shape buildings also define how each visual element is composed.

The identity extends seamlessly across the entire event experience, from core brand elements to on-site collateral — preserving a sense of structural coherence across every application and scale.
